# Guest Access Policy



\## Purpose

Define secure onboarding and management of external users.



\## Scope

All vendor, contractor, and partner accounts.



\## Controls



\### Invitation

\- Only Guest Inviter or Admin roles may invite



\### Authorization

\- Access via groups only

\- No direct permissions



\### Least Privilege

\- Separate groups per application



\### Directory Protection

\- Guests cannot browse tenant users



\### Lifecycle

\- Review every 90 days

\- Disable inactive accounts

\- Delete after project completion



\## Ownership

IAM Team
